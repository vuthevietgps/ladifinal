# Mo ta he thong va muc tieu kinh doanh

## 1. Tong quan he thong
School Management la nen tang quan tri trung tam giao duc theo mo hinh all-in-one, gom van hanh hoc vu, tai chinh, sale-marketing, cham soc phu huynh va quan tri noi bo tren cung mot he thong.

- Backend: NestJS
- Frontend: Angular
- CSDL: MongoDB
- Trien khai: Docker

## 2. Pham vi chuc nang da san sang
He thong hien tai da co do phu nghiep vu rong, bao gom:

1. Quan ly nguoi dung va phan quyen theo vai tro (Director, Ops, Accounting, Teacher, Parent, Sale...).
2. Quan ly hoc vien, lop hoc, lich hoc, diem danh, buoi hoc va bao cao hoc tap.
3. Quan ly hoa don, vi phu huynh, thu chi, luong giao vien, luong nhan su.
4. Quan ly lead, don hang, theo doi hieu qua ads va chatbot da kenh.
5. Nhat ky he thong (audit log), thong bao, xuat bao cao tong hop.

## 3. Gia tri ban hang noi bat
He thong giai quyet truc tiep cac bai toan kinh doanh cua trung tam:

1. Dong bo du lieu xuyen suot tu Lead -> Dang ky -> Hoc tap -> Thu chi -> Bao cao.
2. Giam phu thuoc Excel va thao tac thu cong, han che sai sot doi soat.
3. Minh bach dong tien va hieu suat van hanh theo thoi gian thuc.
4. Tang toc do xu ly van de phu huynh hoc vien nho quy trinh ro rang.
5. San sang mo rong da co so voi quy trinh thong nhat.

## 4. Muc tieu he thong de chao ban
1. So hoa toan bo van hanh trung tam tu sale den hoc vu va tai chinh.
2. Tang doanh thu nho quan tri pipeline sale da kenh va giam that thoat lead.
3. Kiem soat tai chinh theo thoi gian thuc: doanh thu, chi phi, cong no, luong.
4. Chuan hoa phan quyen va quy trinh phe duyet giua cac phong ban.
5. Nang cao trai nghiem phu huynh: minh bach lich hoc, diem danh, hoc phi, tien do.
6. Tang kha nang quan tri rui ro bang audit log va truy vet thao tac.
7. Xay dung nen tang mo rong dai han cho chuoi trung tam.

## 5. KPI muc tieu (6 thang sau trien khai)
1. Giam 30-50% thoi gian van hanh thu cong.
2. Giam 40-60% sai sot nghiep vu (hoa don, diem danh, luong, doi soat).
3. Tang 15-25% ty le chuyen doi lead thanh don.
4. 100% bao cao quan tri van hanh/tai chinh co the truy xuat theo vai tro trong ngay.

## 6. Khuyen nghi de san sang enterprise
De chot cac khach hang yeu cau cao, nen bo sung nhanh 4 hang muc:

1. Chuan hoa bao mat trien khai (bo hard-code secrets, tach env production, quan ly key an toan).
2. Tach du lieu demo khoi moi truong production.
3. Tang cuong kiem soat webhook va chinh sach van hanh bao mat.
4. Hoan thien tai lieu san pham va bo demo theo tung nganh khach hang muc tieu.
