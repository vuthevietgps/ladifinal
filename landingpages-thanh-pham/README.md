## Quy uoc
- Moi landing page nam trong 1 thu muc rieng (vi du: `phu-hieu-xe-check-new/`).
- Cau truc bat buoc theo `QUYTAC.md`:

  - `css/style.css`
  - `js/script.js`
  - `images/`
- Duong dan asset dung dang tuyet doi va de trong cung thu muc landing page.
